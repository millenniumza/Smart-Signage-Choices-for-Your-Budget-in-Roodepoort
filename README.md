## 3. Material Breakdown

### 3.1 Chromadek
- ✔ Affordable  
- ✔ Strong steel sheet  
- ✔ Great for outdoor use  

**Ideal for:** estates, shops, directional signs.

---

### 3.2 Acrylic
- ✔ Modern and clean look  
- ✔ Perfect for indoor branding  
- ✔ Can be laser-cut or 3D mounted  

**Ideal for:** receptions, office branding.

---

### 3.3 Aluminium
- ✔ Superior durability  
- ✔ Weather-resistant  
- ✔ Perfect for long-term installs  

**Ideal for:** corporate parks, exterior wayfinding.

---

### 3.4 PVC Banners
- ✔ Most affordable option  
- ✔ Fast turnaround time  
- ✔ Lightweight and portable  

**Ideal for:** events and short-term promotions.

## 6. Tips to Save Money Without Sacrificing Quality
- ✔ Choose matte laminate for longer outdoor durability  
- ✔ Combine materials (acrylic letters on aluminium)  
- ✔ Use LEDs for lower power costs  
- ✔ Keep design simple to reduce manufacturing time  
- ✔ Take correct measurements to avoid reprints
